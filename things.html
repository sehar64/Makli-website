Things to Do in Makli Thatta

1. Explore the Makli Necropolis

🕌 Walk through one of the world’s largest graveyards, discovering intricately designed tombs and centuries-old history.

2. Photography at Shah Jahan Mosque

📸 Capture the mesmerizing blue-tiled architecture of this Mughal masterpiece, known for its stunning acoustics.

3. Sunset at Keenjhar Lake (Kalri Lake)

🌅 Enjoy a breathtaking sunset at this serene lake, go for a boat ride, or relax by the shore with traditional Sindhi food.

4. Discover the Ruins of Banbhore

🏛️ Walk through the remains of an ancient city believed to be the site where Muhammad Bin Qasim entered Sindh.

5. Visit the Sacred Shrines

🕋 Pay respects at the shrines of Sufi saints like Pir Patho and Jhok Sharif, experiencing the spiritual essence of Thatta.

6. Experience Sindhi Culture in Thatta Bazaar

🛍️ Shop for traditional Sindhi Ajrak, handicrafts, and local sweets at the bustling Thatta market.

7. Bird Watching at Keenjhar Lake

🦆 Spot migratory birds and unique wildlife at one of Sindh’s most important bird sanctuaries.

8. Explore the Abandoned Kalan Kot Fort

🏰 Walk through the ruins of this ancient fort, once a strategic military stronghold in Sindh.

9. Try Local Sindhi Cuisine

🍛 Enjoy authentic Sindhi dishes like Sindhi Biryani, Saag, and Machli (fish) at local dhabas.

10. Attend a Local Festival or Mela

🎶 Experience the vibrant colors, folk music, and traditional dances at cultural events held in and around Thatta.
